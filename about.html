Who We Are

Pixanta Energy Systems

Welcome to a EMPOWERING ENERGY SOLUTIONS

Pixanta is combination of two words Pixel and Quanta. We intend to solve energy problems and develop innovations through design thinking application.

Pixanta Energy is a renewable energy company providing clean energy solutions to Residential, Government, Industrial and Commercial customers. We offer ON grid, OFF grid and Hybrid Solar power plant as per the customer's need.

We are authorized to install all industry leading brands like - Adani Solar, Warree, Vikram, Jakson, Atum, SunEdison, BlueBird and APN.

Our focus is to provide quality solution at an affordable price with an unmatched service management.

To develop best design for your installation and to provide financial solution we have partnered with Oorjan, AHA! Solar, and Heavean's Design.
